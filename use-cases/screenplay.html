Screenplay Script — from beats to call sheets

Map acts → sequences → scenes, then flow into stripboard scheduling and call sheets.

Workflow

  1. Beat Sheet — 8–12 anchor beats with timing.
  2. Scene Matrix — auto IDs, INT/EXT, DAY/NIGHT, location, characters.
  3. Dialogue & Action — side panel for quick drafting (exports to .txt).
  4. Stripboard — plan shoot order by location/time with color tags.
  5. Call Sheets — auto-populate cast/props/locations per scene.

Exports

  • PDF: beat sheet, scene list, schedule, call sheets
  • CSV: scenes & metadata
  • TXT: dialogue snippets
